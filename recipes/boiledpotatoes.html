Boiled Potatoes

Description

Meet the always tasty baked potatoes

Goes well with meat or fish

Ingredients

  • 5-10 potatoes
  • 1 litre of water
  • green stuff

Steps

  1. Put the water on a kettle
  2. Add the potatoes
  3. Boil until the potatoes are soft enough
  4. Throw away the water
  5. Add the green stuff
